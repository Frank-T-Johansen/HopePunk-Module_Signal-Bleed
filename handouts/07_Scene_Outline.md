# Signal Bleed: Scene Outline

## Scene 1: Arrival at Mercy Twelve

The PCs arrive at the clinic for a simple protection job.

Visible tensions:

- patients in the waiting room
- gang underlings near the rear exit
- clinic volunteers trying to keep order
- Bluewire pacing too close to the treatment wing
- the lights flicker every time the relay pulses

## Scene 2: Keep the Peace

A disagreement breaks out over who controls access to the relay.

Options:

- talk down underlings
- investigate Bluewire’s condition
- discover clinic/gang history
- inspect security
- detect corp surveillance
- examine relay symptoms

## Scene 3: Faction Negotiation

The PCs can interact with Dr. Valez, Mara, gang underlings, clinic staff, school volunteers, and corporate intermediaries.

Possible discoveries:

- Mara secretly supports the school and food programs
- the relay contains more than evidence
- the courier’s route should have been impossible
- someone has already tagged the clinic for recovery
- Bluewire’s implants are reacting to the signal

## Scene 4: Corporate Pressure

Commander Rusk or his team makes contact through a drone warning, legal threat, extraction demand, offer of payment, staged power outage, or recovery squad outside.

## Scene 5: Signal Breach

The relay pulses. Lights bloom white-blue. Implants ache. Patients seize or speak in static. AR overlays show impossible geometry. Something hears the signal and answers.

Earlier choices matter.

## Scene 6: First Ascension

The PCs are exposed to the breach and trigger their first Samurai potential.

Focus on protecting civilians, impossible pressure, personal choice, and hope under threat.

## Scene 7: Finale

Possible finale locations:

- treatment wing
- generator room
- roof transmitter
- street outside clinic
- school annex

Final choices:

- broadcast evidence now, risking unstable signal effects
- delay and stabilize it, giving the corp time
- destroy the relay to save the clinic
- give/shared custody to clinic, gang, or corp
- turn the signal into bait for the alien threat
