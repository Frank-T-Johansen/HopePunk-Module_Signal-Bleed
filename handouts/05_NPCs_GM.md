# NPCs: GM Notes

## Dr. Sera Valez

Clinic director, community organizer, moral center. Charismatic through warmth and presence rather than threat.

**Wants:** keep patients alive, verify the signal, expose the corp if possible, prevent the clinic from becoming a battlefield.

**Offers:** medical care, information, local trust, neutral ground, access to the relay.

**Secret:** knows Mara covertly funds food and school support.

## Mara “Mother Red” Vey

Leader of the Redline Choir. Charismatic, controlled, dangerous, and rational.

**Wants:** neighborhood control over the relay, proof against the corp, protection for her people, respect.

**Offers:** local routes, lookouts, fighters, stolen codes, street legitimacy.

**Secret:** covertly funds school meals, medicine, teacher stipends, and generator fuel.

## Nox “Bluewire” Kade

Unstable Redline Choir enforcer. On opioids, overclocked combat ware, and too much cyber strain.

**Wants:** respect, pain relief, proof he is still useful, to stop feeling afraid, to hurt the corp before the corp hurts them.

**Triggers:** mockery, public disarmament, corp uniforms/drones, people touching his cyberware, signal pulses, withdrawal symptoms, sudden movement.

**Important:** Bluewire is not the gang. If he dies, the gang does not automatically attack.

## Commander Ilan Rusk

Corporate recovery commander. Calm, professional, and legally protected.

**Wants:** recover the relay, suppress evidence, prevent visible contamination, avoid corporate blame.

**Offers:** safe passage, money, medical supplies, minor warrant erasure, temporary ceasefire.

## Keet

School volunteer / witness. Young, quick-talking, frightened, and observant.

**Offers:** witness details, hidden route, clue about Mara’s school support.

## Sister Luma

Clinic volunteer and spiritual counselor. Calm, perceptive, and emotionally direct.

**Offers:** emotional reads, patient trust, help calming civilians, spiritual interpretation of the breach.

## Tamsin Quill

Damaged courier. Alive, barely conscious, or recently dead depending on pacing.

**Offers:** final warning, route clue, partial passphrase, contamination symptoms.

## Mote Swarm

First alien pressure. Not a negotiable NPC.

Use to force cooperation, evacuation, and first ascension.
