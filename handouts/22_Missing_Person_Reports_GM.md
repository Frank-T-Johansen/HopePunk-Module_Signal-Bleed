# Missing Person Reports and Biomass Trail

## Purpose

The missing-person trail is how the PCs discover that the corporation’s cleanup failed. The second hidden nest has produced a few juvenile Model 3s. They hunt isolated biomass, kill or disable it, and drag it back to the nest.

The victims are mostly people who move through quiet service routes or are easy to dismiss: maintenance workers, night cleaners, food delivery volunteers, undocumented shelter residents, Redline lookouts, and clinic support staff.

Corporate Recovery initially dismisses the disappearances as gang violence, vagrancy, panic, or people fleeing the district. Redline Choir suspects something is wrong but does not know it is Antithesis.

## Missing: Oskar Venn

**Role:** maintenance worker  
**Last seen:** C6 HVAC / Air Handling  
**Likely status:** dead, partly incorporated into the hidden nest

Hooks:

- Went to check a rattling vent.
- Radio cut out after he said: “Something is breathing in here.”
- His tool cart is still near C6.
- His access badge was used once after he vanished, but only to open a maintenance subdoor.
- Drag marks lead toward a hidden maintenance cavity.
- A boot with no foot inside is found near a warm vent junction.

## Missing: Laleh “Lala” Mir

**Role:** food distribution volunteer  
**Last seen:** B10 Back Service Corridor  
**Likely status:** dead or cocooned near the hidden nest

Hooks:

- Carrying meal crates from a Redline supply route.
- Her crate spilled near a service hatch.
- Food packets are torn open, but not eaten normally.
- A child says she heard “dog feet in the wall.”
- Redline Choir thinks she may have been grabbed by corp or rival gang actors.

## Missing: Bex Aranda

**Role:** Redline lookout  
**Last seen:** A13 Service Corridor / C1 Service Street  
**Likely status:** dead

Hooks:

- Supposedly deserted their post.
- Redline underlings are angry because desertion makes them look weak.
- Switch has footage showing a fast, low shape and Bex being dragged upward into a service gap.
- Mara wants proof before admitting fear in front of outsiders.

## Missing: Narin Pell

**Role:** undocumented shelter resident  
**Last seen:** B8 Shelter Dorm / B12 Emergency Stair  
**Likely status:** possibly alive early, dead later if PCs delay

Hooks:

- Nobody reported it officially because Narin feared authorities.
- Sister Luma knows Narin would not leave without their bag.
- Their bag is still under a bunk.
- A faint organic smear is found near a stair landing.
- Someone heard a muffled scream but assumed it was a fight.

## Missing: Technician Halden Rook

**Role:** corporate cleanup technician / researcher  
**Last seen:** old nest sterilization site  
**Likely status:** dead, evidence trail remains

Hooks:

- Corporate records list him as reassigned.
- Relay logs show he flagged “unexpected Model 1 displacement.”
- His final note: “They’re not fleeing. They’re relocating.”
- His warning was overruled as panic, trauma, or contamination stress.

## Physical clue ladder

### Far from nest

- missing people
- nervous workers
- camera gaps
- unexplained no-shows
- vent rattles
- sweet, warm, organic smell

### Near routes to nest

- three-part bite wounds
- claw marks at knee height
- drag marks
- torn clothing
- acid-scored screws
- dead rats, insects, or birds fused into residue
- warm wet air from cold vents

### Near nest

- Model 3 tracks
- clumps of dead Model 1s dissolved into growing biomass
- partially absorbed victims
- humming or wet clicking
- juveniles dragging prey
- thin nest tissue spreading along ducts and insulation

## Failure mode

If the PCs ignore missing-person clues, escalate instead of stalling:

- a Model 3 attacks a visible NPC
- a Redline lookout vanishes during play
- a corp trooper is dragged away mid-argument
- the ventilation system coughs organic matter into a public area
- the nest reaches a threshold and releases more Model 1s
