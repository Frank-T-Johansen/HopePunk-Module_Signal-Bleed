# Mercy Twelve Clinic

## Player-facing summary

Mercy Twelve is a community clinic, shelter, night-school annex, and last-resort triage station built into an old municipal health building.

The lobby smells of antiseptic, cheap noodles, rain-wet concrete, and overheated batteries. Every wall has been patched more than once. Half the lights flicker. The other half are covered with children’s drawings, hand-written instructions, missing-person notes, and old protest posters.

Mercy Twelve is not safe because the law protects it.

Mercy Twelve is safe because the neighborhood has agreed that some doors should stay open.

## Key areas

### Waiting Room

Crowded with patients, volunteers, gang lookouts pretending not to be lookouts, and at least one child trying to do homework on the floor.

### Triage Hall

Staff move fast here. Outsiders standing in the wrong place will be redirected firmly but politely.

### Treatment Wing

Locked after the courier arrived. Contains the contaminated relay/data-core and at least one patient exposed to it.

### School Annex

Used for night classes, food distribution, tutoring, and shelter overflow.

### Roof Access

Possible drone landing, signal access, escape route, or final transmitter location.

### Generator Room

The clinic’s generator and power storage are old, overloaded, and sensitive to signal pulses.

## Clinic leader

**Dr. Sera Valez** runs Mercy Twelve.

She is warm, direct, exhausted, and hard to intimidate. She does not want a fight. She also will not hand frightened people over to the corp just because someone with a badge and a contract says “compliance.”
