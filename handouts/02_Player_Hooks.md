# Player Hooks: Why Are You at Mercy Twelve?

Choose one, roll one, or make your own with the GM.

## General hooks

1. **Hired Guard** — You were paid to help protect the clinic during a tense data handoff.
2. **Owed Favor** — The clinic patched you up once and never asked who shot you. Now Dr. Valez is calling in the favor.
3. **Local Connection** — Someone you care about lives nearby, works at the clinic, attends the school, or depends on the clinic’s medicine.
4. **Courier Contact** — You knew the courier who brought in the device, or you were supposed to meet them.
5. **Gang Contact** — Mara’s people asked you to attend as an outside witness, specialist, or neutral party.
6. **Corp Trouble** — The corporation involved has hurt you before. You want proof.
7. **Paid Specialist** — You were brought in for a specific skill: hacking, driving, medicine, security, repairs, negotiation, or investigation.
8. **Wrong Place, Right Time** — You came to the clinic for help, shelter, medicine, repairs, or information, and got pulled into the crisis.

## Background-specific ideas

**Bounty Hunter:** You were hired to watch for the missing courier or identify who is tracking the clinic.

**Cat Burglar:** You were asked to quietly check whether someone has already planted a tracker or listening device inside the clinic.

**Corporate Security:** Dr. Valez wants someone who understands corporate recovery protocols.

**Cyber-Junkie:** The damaged relay is screaming across the mesh in ways only you can feel.

**Desert Courier:** You knew the courier route. The person who delivered the device should not have reached the clinic alive.

**Ex-Military:** You were hired to assess the clinic’s defenses and evacuate civilians if violence starts.

**Gambler on the Run:** You owe the clinic, Mara, or the courier. This was supposed to be a quick paid favor.

**Grease Monkey:** The clinic’s generator, ambulance, cargo lift, or relay hardware is failing.

**Gutter Rat Orphan:** You know the back routes, roof gaps, underways, and which kids saw the courier arrive.

**Mesh Hacker:** You were hired to verify, decrypt, or isolate the signal.

**Pilot:** The clinic may need an emergency extraction, rooftop pickup, or drone/vehicle support.

**Piss-Poor Artist:** You are connected to the local school, mural project, protest network, or underground broadcast scene.

**Religious Fanatic:** You believe the clinic, the suffering patients, or the signal has spiritual significance.

**Rogue Surgeon:** Dr. Valez needs medical help with a patient exposed to the device.

**Samurai Otaku:** You came because rumors say the signal contains real Samurai telemetry.

**Scavenger:** You recognize the relay hardware as something salvaged from a forbidden zone.

**Student Dropout:** You know the schoolkids, the community tutors, or the student mesh network that first noticed the signal.

**Test Subject:** The signal causes symptoms that feel horribly familiar.

**Underground Fighter:** You were hired as visible muscle, or a local kid from the clinic follows your fights.

**Washed Up Rocker:** The clinic wants your public voice, old contacts, or ability to calm a crowd.
