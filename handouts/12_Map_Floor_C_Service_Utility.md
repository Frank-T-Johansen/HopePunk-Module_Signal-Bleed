# Map: Floor C — Service, Utility, and Maintenance Floor

## Function

This floor is the megacomplex’s practical underside.

It contains the systems that keep Mercy Twelve and the surrounding district alive: power, air, water, cargo, repair, storage, and maintenance access.

It is more industrial than the clinic and shelter maps, but still part of the Hope//Punk environment. The people here keep the lights on.

## What the map shows

Key areas:

- logistics/service street
- central loading or distribution zone
- generator or power room
- battery banks
- HVAC / air handling
- water treatment or recycling
- storage rooms
- repair workshop
- monitoring/security office
- elevator core
- emergency stair / ladderwell
- suspicious side room or hidden service space

## Scenario use

Good scenes here:

- power failure caused by signal pulses
- Grease Monkey or Scavenger gets useful spotlight
- corp team tries to cut the clinic’s systems
- gang uses maintenance routes to bypass public areas
- players discover a hidden route between maps
- alien contamination starts in the infrastructure
- Bluewire’s implants react near power or signal conduits

## Tactical role

This floor is good for:

- stealth routes
- chases
- sabotage
- repairs
- ambushes
- alternate access
- restoring power
- isolating the relay
- moving unseen between public areas

## Social role

Do not make this floor empty. Maintenance workers, delivery crews, unofficial vendors, Redline Choir lookouts, and tired clinic staff may all use it.

It is a good place for non-faction passersby who are just trying to work.

## Elevator and stair notes

The elevator is likely a cargo-capable lift here.

The stair/ladderwell is probably a steep maintenance access route. It can bypass shutdowns, but moving wounded people through it should require time, equipment, or checks.
