# Supporting NPCs and Spies

These NPCs are intended as map tokens, conversation targets, suspects, witnesses, and pressure valves. They are less important than the faction heads, but they make the maps feel populated and give players people to talk to.

## Mercy Twelve Clinic

### Senior Nurse Imani Cho

**Token name:** Nurse Cho  
**Location:** A3 Reception or A5 Triage  
**Role:** clinic floor lead  
**Use:** practical guide, triage authority, patient-protection voice

Hooks:

- knows which patients cannot be evacuated quickly
- knows Lala Mir by name from food distribution
- suspects recent “no-shows” are connected
- can authorize access to non-private clinic logs if Dr. Valez agrees

Secret pressure:

- terrified that the relay proves the clinic unknowingly treated illegal trial subjects

### Orderly Pax Ruun

**Token name:** Pax Ruun  
**Location:** A6 Emergency Intake  
**Role:** nervous witness  
**Use:** emergency-bay clue source

Hooks:

- saw unusual patient transfers
- recognizes a supposedly discharged patient from relay files
- knows the courier did not arrive through the official bay
- may panic if Corporate Recovery enters the clinic

Secret pressure:

- deleted one minor intake note because he feared being blamed

### Rafa Mbeki

**Token name:** Rafa Mbeki or Clinic Security  
**Location:** A10 Records / clinic security alcove  
**Role:** clinic camera monitor  
**Use:** control-room cooperation point

Hooks:

- can pull clinic camera feeds
- knows privacy-disabled rooms
- noticed missing footage near patient transfers
- can compare clinic doors with Redline street cameras if Switch cooperates

Secret pressure:

- sat on some camera anomalies because reporting them would invite corporate seizure

### Dr. Vela Myung

**Token name:** Dr. Myung  
**Location:** A7 Trauma / A9 Recovery  
**Role:** tired trauma doctor  
**Use:** medical interpretation of exposure-trial victims

Hooks:

- can identify false treatment protocols in relay files
- notices that some “medicine” had trial-batch codes
- can identify Model 3 bite trauma once shown a victim

Secret pressure:

- once accepted corporate-donated medicine without asking enough questions

## Redline Choir

### Vex Tan

**Token name:** Vex Tan or Redline Runner  
**Location:** B4 Pantry / B10 Service Corridor  
**Role:** supply runner  
**Use:** aid-route witness

Hooks:

- moved food and medicine through unofficial routes
- knows Lala’s normal route
- can confirm Mara ordered no shooting near the clinic
- can show where Redline cameras stop

Secret pressure:

- smuggles some personal side goods; worried PCs will mistake that for the main crime

### Juno “Switch” Hale

**Token name:** Switch  
**Location:** B10 Back Corridor or C9 Monitoring  
**Role:** Redline camera sitter  
**Use:** surveillance witness and spy

Hooks:

- has footage of Bex being dragged toward a service gap
- knows Redline blind spots
- can compare camera angles with clinic feeds
- saw corp biohazard containers moving before the current incident

Spy status:

- **Spy for Corporate Recovery, by pressure.**
- Switch sold selected feed access to a corporate handler to pay medical debt / protect someone in Redline territory.
- Switch did not know about the second nest and did not intend to get people killed.
- If exposed, Switch can still become useful if offered protection or a way out.

### Rook “Mads” Madsen

**Token name:** Mads  
**Location:** A1 Indoor Street / A13 Service Corridor  
**Role:** Redline underling  
**Use:** suspicious muscle with useful local knowledge

Hooks:

- knows Bex did not seem like a deserter
- can point toward service routes
- recognizes corp observers by posture
- can be talked into evacuation help if Bex’s fate is proven

Secret pressure:

- hates Bluewire and may escalate if Bluewire is humiliated

### Talla “Auntie’s Eyes” Vey

**Token name:** Talla  
**Location:** B3 Commons / B4 Pantry  
**Role:** Mara loyalist and aid coordinator  
**Use:** protects Mara’s hidden soft power

Hooks:

- knows who receives food and medicine
- knows Lala, Miri, and Sol
- can confirm Mara funds school meals if PCs earn trust
- will deny everything if asked like an accusation

Secret pressure:

- not actually family, despite the surname; uses it because Mara saved her years ago

## Corporate Recovery

### Lt. Varya Senn

**Token name:** Lt. Senn  
**Location:** A1 Concourse / C1 Service Street  
**Role:** Rusk’s field second  
**Use:** tactical pressure and escalation

Hooks:

- more aggressive than Rusk
- wants a clean seizure before crowds form
- can recognize Model 3 evidence as impossible if she saw old cleanup files
- may accept a temporary ceasefire only after proof of the second nest

Secret pressure:

- believes witness control is ugly but necessary

### Orlan Pike

**Token name:** Feed Handler or Bloke #3  
**Location:** off-map, C9 Monitoring, or D6 Control Room  
**Role:** corporate surveillance handler  
**Use:** spy, footage editor, evidence suppressor

Hooks:

- edits camera footage
- injects false timestamps
- bought access from Switch
- can be identified by mesh/camera forensics
- tries to frame Redline Choir for disappearances

Spy status:

- **Spy inside local systems for Corporate Recovery, by greed and career ambition.**
- He is the handler pressuring Switch.
- If exposed, Rusk may deny authorizing him, even if Rusk benefits from his work.

### Corp Recovery Pair

**Token names:** Corp Recovery #1, Corp Recovery #2  
**Location:** A1, C1, or D3  
**Role:** disciplined mooks  
**Use:** tactical and moral pressure

Hooks:

- one can be dragged by a Model 3 to prove the threat is real
- one may break discipline to save a civilian
- Small Arms / Tactics can read their formation and intent

Secret pressure:

- they were briefed on witness cleanup, not on an active second nest

### Mara Silex

**Token name:** Corp Medic  
**Location:** A1 / C1 / D3  
**Role:** corporate field medic  
**Use:** uneasy corporate conscience

Hooks:

- recognizes exposure-treatment batch codes
- can stabilize victims
- knows some corporate medical orders are indefensible
- may quietly help if civilians are at risk

Spy status:

- **Potential Mercy Twelve informant, by conscience.**
- She has leaked fragments to Dr. Valez before, but not enough to expose herself.
- Can become an ally if PCs protect her from Rusk/Senn.

## Recommended spy placement

Use at least two:

```text
Switch → compromised by Corporate Recovery, pressured by medical debt.
Orlan Pike → corporate surveillance handler, greedy/careerist.
Mara Silex → corporate medic leaking to Mercy Twelve by conscience.
```

For a shorter game, use only Switch and Pike.

For a richer game, add Silex as a possible redemption/contact NPC.

## Spy pressure on the Valez/Vey relationship

Orlan Pike can use the Sera/Mara relationship as blackmail material.

Possible plays:

- leak that Mercy Twelve is “gang-compromised”
- imply Sera knowingly launders Redline aid
- suggest Mara is hiding behind her sister’s clinic
- pressure Switch to deliver footage proving Mara’s aid routes pass through clinic-adjacent spaces
- pressure Mara Silex to stop quietly helping the clinic

This should make the spy subplot feel tied to the main social conflict, not like a disconnected side mystery.
