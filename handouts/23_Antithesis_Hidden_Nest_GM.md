# Antithesis in Signal Bleed: Model 1s, Model 3s, and the Hidden Nest

## Module-specific Antithesis structure

Signal Bleed uses a small local nest, not a full-scale invasion.

### Model 1s

Use Model 1s mainly as backstory and late-stage pressure.

In this module:

- the original corporate nest produced Model 1s
- corporate sterilization nearly wiped them out
- several Model 1s escaped in formation
- they suicided in a clump at a hidden site
- their bodies seeded the second nest

The strange Model 1 behavior is the key clue that the corporate cleanup failed.

### Model 3 juveniles

Use a few juvenile Model 3s as the active present-tense threat.

They are dog-like Antithesis predators with jaws that split into three parts. Their purpose here is not to conquer openly, but to gather biomass for the hidden nest.

Behavior:

```text
stalk isolated prey
ambush from low angles
disable or kill quickly
drag biomass back to the nest
avoid unnecessary fights with strong groups
retreat through vents, service shafts, and maintenance gaps
become bolder as the nest grows
```

## Tactical behavior

A Model 3 juvenile should try to:

- attack isolated targets
- knock prey down
- drag unconscious or dead victims away
- retreat if badly hurt
- attack civilians, wounded NPCs, lone guards, and panicked targets first
- use vents/service routes that humans find awkward
- avoid fighting Samurai-capable PCs to the death unless cornered or defending the nest

## Horror tell

The first visible reveal should be the jaw.

Examples:

```text
Its head opens in three directions.
For one second it looks like a dog.
Then its face unfolds.
The jaw splits like a wet flower with teeth.
```

## Encounter pacing

### First sign — no combat

- vent cover bent outward
- three-pronged bite marks
- drag marks toward a service hatch
- a food crate spilled and smeared with organic residue
- a camera shows one impossible low blur
- someone’s shoe or badge is found near a duct

### First glimpse — brief contact

- something low and fast crosses the end of a corridor
- a jaw opens wrong in a camera freeze-frame
- a child says “it was like a dog, but the mouth was wrong”
- Bluewire or another unstable NPC hears clicking in the walls

### First fight — rescue pressure

Best first fight setup:

```text
A Model 3 juvenile has grabbed an NPC and is trying to drag them into a service route.
```

Good victims:

- Redline Lookout #2
- Food Line Volunteer
- Orderly Pax Ruun
- Corp Recovery #2
- Narin Pell
- random “Bloke #3” token

This teaches the players the Model 3 goal: it wants biomass, not a fair fight.

### Nest reveal

The hidden nest should be small but horrifying:

- dead Model 1 clump forming the seed
- warm wet tissue along ducts
- bones/tools/badges partly absorbed
- victims used as scaffolding
- one possibly savable victim if the PCs were fast
- juvenile Model 3s defending or feeding it
- signs that it will produce more Antithesis if not stopped

## Human faction reaction

### Clinic

Horrified, but immediately focused on triage, evacuation, and exposure risk.

### Redline Choir

Angry and frightened because their people were taken. This can push them into cooperation if the PCs respect them.

### Corporate Recovery

Initially dismissive or hostile. Once the second nest is proven, Rusk has to choose between admitting the cleanup failed, helping contain the new nest, doubling down on witness control, or trying to destroy everything and blame the neighborhood.

## Skill prompts

- **Medicine / Surgery:** identify bite trauma, biomass incorporation, whether a victim can be saved.
- **Tracking / Bounty Hunter:** follow drag marks, identify prey movement, find ambush routes.
- **Small Arms:** read where shots were fired at something low and fast.
- **Big Guns:** identify corp sterilization damage from the old nest.
- **Blades / Melee:** read close-quarters defensive wounds and jaw-strike patterns.
- **Grease Monkey:** identify duct access, damaged vents, and maintenance routes.
- **Mesh Hacker:** retrieve camera frames and access logs; this is human surveillance, not alien electronics.
- **Streetwise:** know which missing people would not have left voluntarily.
- **Psychology / Empathy:** calm witnesses who saw the jaw unfold.
